## Entry Points for Contributors

**Start anywhere. Fork freely. No permission needed.**

### Story Entry Points

- Write a letter from Éloïse to Julien from five years after their split, one she never sent.
- Describe a day in the life of the housekeeper at the Manoir de la Lune, observing the two philosophers.
- Write the 'final piece of business' Éloïse wants to complete, from her perspective.
- Create a new chapter where Julien discovers a hidden cache of Éloïse's letters to other thinkers.

### Technical Entry Points

- Build an interactive map of the Manoir de la Lune and its cliffside setting.
- Create a web app that presents the novel as a series of exchanged letters and diary entries, revealing content based on reader choices.
